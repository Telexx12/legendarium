Reformed Church Cotu»ô
The single nave church was built in 1782. The sanctuary has a hexagonal form and it is oriented north-east. The outside walls of the church have modest decorations and curved windows.
In the south-west of the nave is the tower and in the south-east part is the entrance with an arcade.
The inside of the church is puritanical, it is painted in white. The ceiling is decorated with stucco frame, the nave also has floral decorations.
